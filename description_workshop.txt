# Gilded Blade Wings

A unique wings mod with growth mechanics and auto-save features for Terraria.

## Key Features

### Wings Growth System
- Wings grow stronger as you defeat bosses
- Each boss defeat increases flight time by 0.5 seconds
- Horizontal flight speed improves with each boss defeated
- Additional stat boosts per boss:
  - +5 Max HP
  - +1% All Damage
  - +1% Critical Strike Chance
  - +1 Defense
  - +0.2 Base Attack Damage
  - +0.1 Life Regeneration
  - +5 Max Mana
- Every 5 boss defeats:
  - +1 Minion Slot
  - +1 Sentinel Slot

### Auto-Save System
- Configurable auto-save intervals (60-3600 seconds)
- Auto-save when defeating bosses
- Toggleable auto-save functionality
- Save notifications to keep you informed

### Visual Effects
- Dynamic aura effects
- Glowing textures with multiple layers
- Smooth animations
- Performance optimization with low-fidelity mode

## How to Obtain

Craft at a Workbench with:
- 20 Iron Bars
- 20 Fallen Stars

## Configuration

Access mod settings to:
- Adjust flight parameters
- Toggle low-fidelity mode
- Configure auto-save settings
- Customize your experience

# 耀刃之翼

一个具有成长机制和自动保存功能的独特翅膀模组。

## 主要特性

### 翅膀成长系统
- 翅膀会随着你击败BOSS而变得更强大
- 每击败一个BOSS，飞行时间增加0.5秒
- 水平飞行速度随击败BOSS数量提升
- 每个BOSS提供的额外属性加成：
  - +5 最大生命值
  - +1% 全伤害
  - +1% 暴击率
  - +1 防御力
  - +0.2 基础攻击力
  - +0.1 生命恢复
  - +5 最大魔力
- 每击败5个BOSS：
  - +1 召唤栏
  - +1 哨兵栏

### 自动保存系统
- 可配置的自动保存间隔（60-3600秒）
- 击败BOSS时自动保存
- 可开关的自动保存功能
- 保存通知让你随时了解保存状态

### 视觉效果
- 动态光环效果
- 多层发光纹理
- 流畅的动画
- 低精度模式优化性能

## 获取方式

在工作台合成：
- 20个铁锭
- 20个落星

## 配置

访问模组设置可：
- 调整飞行参数
- 切换低精度模式
- 配置自动保存设置
- 自定义你的游戏体验
